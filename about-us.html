About us

It’s not a coincidence Duy Tan University’s Hospitality &Tourism Management School is located in Da Nang. Da Nang is one of the fastest growing tourism centres in Vietnam with over 5 million visitors in 2017, of which 1.8 million were foreigners (an increase of 25% over 2016!). Together with ancient towns such as Hoi An and Hue, and the traditional villages in the mountains, the Central Region of Vietnam provides many opportunities for tourists to explore.

The Hospitality & Tourism Management School strives to equip their students with current, relevant, industry specific know-how and hands-on experience, providing them the knowledge and practical & soft skills to prosper in their chosen field.

Students of the Hospitality & Tourism Management School have easy access to internships at one of the many 4 and 5 star hotels, resorts, restaurants and tour operators in the region due to our well established connections with local industry. Or they can study abroad at one of the partner universities in Asia and the USA and experience living and studying in a different country.

Our outstanding programs result in 50% of our graduates having a job lined up before they graduate and another 26% finding employment in the industry within 3 months after graduating!

Duy Tan University was the first private university in the Central Region and has been providing high quality education to more than 40,000 students since its inception in 1994.
